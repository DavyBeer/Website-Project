Lower Workouts

  • Straight Leg Raises: Laying on your back, flexone leg to 90 degrees and then raise the opposite legas high as possible while keeping it straight.
  • Wall Slide: Place your back flat against the wall and push up and down with your legs.
  • Squats: With feet shoulder width apart, lower your body until your legs are flexed at 90 degrees, then push to stand up straight.
  • Donkey Kicks: Standing on your hands and knees, kick one leg backwards and up, repeating with the other leg.
  • Bench Hip Thrust: Supporting your upper back on the seat of a chair and your feet flat on the ground, raise and lower your hips.
  • Nordic Hamstring Curl: Kneeling on your knees with someone holding down your feet, lean forward with arms crossed as deep as you can go and come back up.
