  • Crunch: Lay on your back with your knees flexed at 90 degrees and arms to your side, then raise your chest toward your knees without lifting your lower back off the floor, then return to the starting position.
  • Reverse Crunch: lay flat on your back with your hands at your sides, then raise your legs above your hips while keeping them straight, and return to the laying down position.
  • Full Crunch: lay flat on your back with hands on your head, simultaneously lift your legs and your upper body, with both portions reaching above your hips, then return to the laying position.
  • Russian Twist: Balance on your lower back while holding your legs crossed in the air and your upper torso in the air, then twist your upper body from side to side.
  • Bicycle Crunch: Lay on your back with your legs flexed at 90 degrees, then bring opposite elbows and knees together in a repeating fashion.
  • Side Plank: with a straight body, hold your body on its side, supported by an outstretched elbow and your feet, for one minute.
